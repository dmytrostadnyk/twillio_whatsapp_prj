# NovaBrew Coffee — Business Context

## About Us

NovaBrew Coffee is a specialty coffee roastery and café based in Vilnius, Lithuania.
We source single-origin beans from Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, roast them
in small batches every Tuesday and Friday, and ship across Europe within 2–5 business
days. We also have a cozy walk-in café where you can enjoy freshly brewed coffee and
light pastries.

## Shipping & Delivery

- **Lithuania**: free delivery on orders over €30; otherwise €3.50. Delivered in
  1–2 business days via LP Express.
- **EU (excluding Lithuania)**: €8.90 flat rate. Delivered in 3–5 business days
  via DPD or DHL depending on the destination country.
- **Outside EU**: we do not currently ship outside the EU.

Orders are packed in sealed foil bags with a one-way valve to preserve freshness.
Roasted-on date is printed on every bag.

---

## Returns & Refunds

- **Unopened products**: full refund within 14 days of delivery. Contact us to
  arrange free return shipping for orders over €50.
- **Opened products**: we cannot accept returns on opened coffee due to hygiene
  reasons. If your order arrived damaged or incorrect, send a photo to
  hello@novabrew.lt and we will arrange a replacement or full refund within
  2 business days.
- **Brewing equipment**: full refund within 14 days if unused and in original
  packaging. Defective items replaced free of charge within 1 year.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do you offer decaf?**
Not currently, but we are trialling a Swiss Water Process decaf from Brazil —
expected to launch in Q3 2026.

**Can I visit the roastery?**
Yes! Roastery tours run every Saturday at 11:00. Book via our website or email us.

**Which payment methods do you accept?**
Online: Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, and Paysera. In-café: card and cash.

**I have a nut allergy — are your products safe?**
Our roastery does not handle nuts. However, pastries served in the café are
sourced from a third-party bakery that may use nuts. Please ask staff when you
visit.

**How fresh is your coffee?**
We roast twice a week (Tuesday and Friday). We recommend brewing within 2–4 weeks
of the roast date for peak flavour. All bags include the roast date.
